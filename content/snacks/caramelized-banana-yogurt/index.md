### Macros Per Serving (1 Total)
### 380 Calories | 20g Protein | 52g Carbs | 11g Fat

### Ingredients (1 Serving)
- 200g Greek yogurt
- 110g banana, sliced
- 15g walnuts
- 15g honey
- 10g dark chocolate chips
- Cinnamon to taste

### Steps:
1. Slice the banana.
2. Heat a pan over medium heat and add the banana, honey, and cinnamon.
3. Cook for a few minutes, flipping occasionally, until the banana is soft and lightly caramelized.
4. Add the yogurt to a bowl.
5. Top with the caramelized banana, walnuts, and dark chocolate chips.
