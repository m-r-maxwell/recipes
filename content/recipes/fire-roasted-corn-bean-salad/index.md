## Macros Per Serving 
## 383 Calories | 11g Protein | 47g Carbs | 20g Fat

Ingredients (4 Servings)
- 2 bags frozen fire-roasted corn (12 oz each)
- 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 medium cucumber, diced
- 2 red bell peppers, diced
- 1 small shallot, finely minced
- ½ cup crumbled feta cheese
- 1.5 medium avocados, diced (add when serving)
- Juice of 1 lime
- ¼ cup light mayonnaise
- Salt, to taste
- Chili powder, to taste
- Garlic powder, to taste
- Onion powder, to taste

Steps:
1. Cook the frozen corn according to package instructions, preferably roasting or pan-searing to remove excess moisture. Let cool slightly.
2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked corn and black beans.
3. Add the diced cucumber, red bell peppers, minced shallot, and feta cheese.
4. In a small bowl, mix the lime juice, light mayo, salt, chili powder, garlic powder, and onion powder.
5. Pour the dressing over the salad and mix until evenly coated.
6. Cover and refrigerate for meal prep.
7. When serving, dice and add avocado to each portion and gently mix.
