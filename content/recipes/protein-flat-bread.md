### 269 Calories | 30g Protein | 15g Carbs | 15g Fat
### 1 Portion

# Ingredients:
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup cottage cheese 
- Garlic powder 
- Onion powder 
- Italian seasoning 

# Steps:
1. Blend everything together 
2. Spread over parchment paper
3. Bake at 350 for 40 minutes

# Serve:
Use as a wrap or for making a sandwich
