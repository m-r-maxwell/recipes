## Calories: approx 612 per serving Protein: approx 53.1g per serving

## Ingredients (Serves 4):
- 150g bacon, finely diced
- 500g chicken breast, diced
- 2 tsp smoked paprika
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 2 tsp garlic granules
- 1 tsp onion granules
- Salt and black pepper
- 1 onion, finely diced
- 100g tender stem broccoli, finely diced
- 3 garlic cloves, finely grated or 1 tsp garlic purée
- 300ml chicken stock
- 100g Philadelphia cream cheese
- 120g grated cheese
- 4 large flour tortillas or 8 small tortillas, cut into strips 
- Handful of fresh parsley or coriander (optional)

## Steps:
1. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat and add the diced bacon. Cook for 2-3 minutes until golden and crispy, then remove from the pan and set aside.
2. Add the chicken to the same pan, season with the smoked paprika, cumin, garlic granules, onion granules, salt and pepper and cook for 3-4 minutes until sealed and lightly golden.
3. Add the onion and diced tenderstem broccoli and cook for 2–3 minutes until softened, then stir in the garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
4. Pour in the chicken stock and bring to a simmer, scraping up any bits from the bottom of the pan. Lower the heat slightly and stir in the Philadelphia until smooth and creamy.
5. Add in the tortillas to the pan and mix well, scatter over the cheese and bacon and pop on a lid for 2-3 minutes or until the cheese has melted, garlic with fresh parsley or coriander. Serve and enjoy!
